Research Activity:

You will be asked to play a picture naming and matching game with a partner. Today, your partner will be another participant. You will be connected to your partner before you start playing the game.

You and your partner will see matching grids of images. If you see an image with a red box around it, you should ask your partner to click on that object. If you do not see a red box around any image, your partner will ask you to click on an object. You will take turns, alternating between clicking or telling your partner which picture to click.

The aim of this game is to complete the naming and clicking tasks as quickly and accurately as possible.

When it is your turn to ask your partner what to click, press and hold the T key to activate your microphone. Turns will advance each time the clicking partner clicks on an object.

Screen layout:

There will be some objects that only you can see. These will have grey backgrounds for you and be blacked out for your partner.

There are also some objects that only your partner can see. These are blacked out for you and have grey backgrounds for your partner.
